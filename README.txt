The Temple of No
----------------

A Twine game presented by Crows Crows Crows.
Designed and illustrated by Dominik Johann (@zerstoerer).
Written and voice acted by William Pugh (@HonestWilliam).
Additional writing by Joe Finegold (@FinegoldJoe).
Sound design by Tom Schley (@silkersoft).

Produced by Alicia Contestabile (@swizzle_kiss) and Kevin Patterson (@InternalArt).

Ambient sounds by Freesound and Soundsnap. The Dancing Frog Song is Gold Rush by Kevin MacLeod. Boulder scene audio from Raiders of The Lost Ark by Steven Spielberg.

Hosted on itch.io. View the game's source on GitHub.

Created with Twine. Shoutouts to Sean and L!
